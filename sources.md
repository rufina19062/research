# Cold-outreach-pipeline-for-B2B-SaaS

I chose this topic because cold outreach is one of the few channels where you control almost every variable — you can measure it, iterate quickly, and build a repeatable system without a big ad budget. The research focuses on people who actually run campaigns, not just write about them.
 
Selection criteria: verifiable results (reply rates, email volume, real clients, or a relevant career track record), and still actively sharing content in the past 3 months.
