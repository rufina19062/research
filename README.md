# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

## Why I Chose This Topic

I chose cold outreach because it is one of the few growth channels where almost every variable can be controlled and measured. Unlike paid advertising, outbound campaigns can be tested, improved, and scaled without requiring a large budget.

I was also interested in understanding how experienced practitioners build repeatable systems for generating meetings and pipeline through cold email, LinkedIn outreach, and prospecting.

## Research Goal

The goal of this project is to study how successful outbound professionals approach cold outreach in B2B SaaS.

Rather than focusing on theories, I wanted to learn from people who actively run campaigns, work with clients, or share real-world results. By collecting and reviewing their content, I hope to identify common strategies, frameworks, and best practices that consistently appear across different practitioners.

## Selection Criteria

The experts included in this research were selected based on:

* Experience running outbound or lead generation campaigns
* Evidence of real client work, business results, or industry expertise
* Active content sharing within the last three months
* Relevance to B2B SaaS cold outreach and prospecting
